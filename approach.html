Our approach

A golden standard, not a marketing line.

Every family works with Jennifer directly, and every plan is built around the same four commitments.

Every student is accepted

Grades, test scores, and where a student is starting from don't determine whether Starrett Success Center will work with them. Every student gets a plan built for where they actually are.

It's never too late

A changed major, a late application, a gap year, a student who's behind: none of it is disqualifying. There's a path from here, and finding it is the job.

Built on real disability experience

Years spent building individualized learning plans and tracking real progress for students with different learning needs means accommodations and different approaches aren't an afterthought here, they're a starting point.

No room for discrimination

Every student and every family is treated with the same respect, regardless of background, ability, or identity. That's not a policy, it's the reason this business exists.

How it works

A plan built in four steps, then adjusted as needed

Step 1

Free consultation

A conversation, not a sales pitch. Jennifer learns where the student is, what's already been tried, and what the family actually needs, then recommends a starting point.

Step 2

A written plan

Every family leaves with an actual plan on paper: timeline, sessions, and what success looks like, not a vague promise to "figure it out together."

Step 3

Live sessions, wherever the student is

In-person for local Austin-area families, or over Zoom and Microsoft Teams for families anywhere else. The same plan, the same accountability, regardless of zip code.

Step 4

Tracked progress, adjusted as needed

Plans aren't static. Progress gets tracked session to session, and the plan adjusts when the student's needs do.
