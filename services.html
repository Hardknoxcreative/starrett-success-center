College & Career Path

Direction for the student who doesn't know where they're headed, and the student who does but doesn't know how to get there.

College Admissions Consulting

School lists built around fit, not just rankings. Essay strategy and editing. Activities lists that actually tell a story. Application timeline management from freshman year through decision day.

Full package: $900–$1,500 · Hourly: $95/hr

Academic Coaching

Subject-specific homework help beyond math, graduation-plan tracking, and real-world financial and career literacy, built on an MBA in Finance and years of watching students turn "I don't know what I want" into an actual plan.

$75/hr · 5-session package: $340

Executive Functioning Support

Organization, time management, and study systems built for students who need a different approach than what's worked for everyone else, informed by years of building individualized learning plans.

$75/hr · 5-session package: $340

Parent Consultations

A standalone session to map out FAFSA timing, testing strategy, or a school list before committing to a full package, or a check-in built into any ongoing plan.

$60/session · included in most packages

Math Tutoring
